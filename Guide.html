MARINDUQUE STATE UNIVERSITY

SCHOOL GUIDE











CAMPUS


MarSU consists of one main campus located at Boac and three more branches located at Sta. Cruz, Torrijos and Gasan. MarSU provides a learning resource center (library and multimedia services), student publication, Office of the Student Affairs (OSA) student organizations and activities, Guidance and Psychological Testing Services, Health Services (Medical and Dental), Scholarship and Financial Assistance Program, and Sports, Physical education and Recreation (SPEAR). It has services like dormitory, internet, canopy and study sheds, bulletin boards, and speech laboratory.

HISTORY


The university was established in 1952 as the Marinduque School of Arts and Trades. An act in 1983 expanded its programs into Science and Technology, renaming it to Marinduque Institute of Science and Technology. The school was named a college in 1992, by a Republic Act that added Agriculture and Fisheries to its programs, as well as establishing its branch campuses. In 2019, the College announced the legislature had approved its plans to become a university.

ACADEMICS


Marinduque State University has a variety of courses and it was divided by their Campus Branches to municipalities, such as Boac Campus, Sta. Cruz Campus, Gasan Campus, and Torrijos Campus. (except the municipalities of Buenavista and Buenavista). As of 2021, it has received certificates of compliance from the Commission on Higher Education Regional Office IV-B (CHEdRO–Mimamropa) for its academic programs.

Library

The MarSU Learning Resource Center (LRC) is a partnership with MSAT consisting of a main library and three branches. It supports learning and research activities and is supervised by Ms. Arlene M. Rualo. The LRC is upgrading its tools, facilities, and services by acquiring new books and periodicals and improving technical support for readers.

Park

Marinduque State University park is a serene environment that provides a break from campus life. It features well-maintained greenery and tall trees, with benches and seating areas for studying and relaxing. Teachers and students can enjoy picnics and outdoor activities in this beautiful park.
